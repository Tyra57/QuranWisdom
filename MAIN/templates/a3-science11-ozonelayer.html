Scientific Insights


The existence of the "Van Allen Radiation Belt" shield was discovered in 1958 by Dr. James Van Allen (1326 years after the death of the Prophet Muhammad in 632 CE). This shield consists of two belts that block radiation attacks from the sun and harmful gases in space from reaching the Earth.
