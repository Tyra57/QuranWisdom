Quranic Verse

“and feed from ˹the flower of˺ any fruit ˹you please˺ and follow the ways your Lord has made easy for you.” From their bellies comes forth liquid of varying colours, in which there is healing for people. Surely in this is a sign for those who reflect.”


Scientific Insights


Honey is renowned worldwide as a remedy for various diseases or as a pain reliever or symptom reducer for certain illnesses. In ancient times, it was used as a remedy for gastric issues, stomach cramps, problems with taste perception (tongue), reducing coughs, and sore throats.


Only in the 19th century did scientists begin to study honey more in-depth. Honey has an osmotic effect, meaning it contains very low water content, preventing microorganisms such as bacteria/viruses from proliferating. It is also used to treat diabetic ulcer patients. It can also reduce odor, bacterial infection, & scarring effects on wound scars or burn scars, as well as accelerate the healing process of these wounds. Its sweetness is also not as harmful compared to the sweetness of sugar. The pH level of honey is usually between 3.2 - 4.5, which is somewhat acidic, thereby inhibiting the growth of many bacteria/viruses. It also prevents chronic sinusitis, treats bacterial infections in surgical wounds, & reduces ocular allergies.
