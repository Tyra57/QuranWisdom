Scientific Insights


The Quran has narrated the step-by-step creation of man in the womb, while people at that time did not know what happens inside a woman's womb. All the chronology below proves that scientists only began to understand this in the early 1920s..!!


1. Fertilization. The union of two seeds.
2. Embryogenesis, Day 3-7. A clot of blood forms, attaching to the uterine wall, as a result of fertilization.
3. Embryo, Week 5. The flesh of the embryo begins to form. The heart, liver, lungs, leg and arm buds start to form.
4. Embryo, Week 8. Bones, hands, feet, and head begin to form.
5. Fetus, Week 20. Muscles and skin begin to form, covering the body of the fetus.
6. Fetus, Weeks 35-40, the baby has fully developed. Given the senses of taste, hearing & sight (eyes respond).
