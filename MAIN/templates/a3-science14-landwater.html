Ratio Of Land and Water on Earth's Surface



Based on separate research findings by Dr. Tariq Al-Suwaidan (an Islamic scholar at Islamic Gateway) and the notes of author Nadir As-Sayyid Husein Ad-Dawik in his book, Al-I’jazul Adadi Fil Quranil Karim. The word for water/sea is mentioned 32 times in the Quran. The word for land is mentioned 13 times in the Quran.



Al-Quran


Water/sea = 32 times
Land = 13 times

The Earth's surface contains both water and land. So, to get the full ratio value of the Earth's surface, the sum of water and land must be added.

Water + Land = 32+13 = 45 = total Earth's surface

Percentage of water on Earth's surface according to the Quran = (32/45) × 100% = 71.111%

Percentage of land on Earth's surface according to the Quran = (13/45) × 100% = 28.889%
